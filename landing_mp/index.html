Hacks/Hackers Buenos Aires

The Media Party is a three days event in Argentina, that brings together 2500 entrepreneurs, journalists, software programmers and designers from five continents to work together for the future of media. Born in Hacks/Hackers Buenos Aires, the event merges big companies like New York Times, The Guardian, Vox, ProPublica, Watchup, Neo4J or DocumentCloud plus the regional communities of the biggest network of journalists and developers in the world.
